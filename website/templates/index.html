Early life


I was born on 30 April 1966.during my early years we moved a lot due to my father's business needs and I effectively lived in Johannesburg, East London, Durban, Cape Town and again Johannesburg, all by the time I started High School.

In High School I went to boarding school, namely Queen's College in the Eastern Cape. It is there I met some of the best friends anyone could have. After matriculating at the end of 1983, I went nd did my national service for two years. Once again, I met great buddies, one of whom is possibly the best mate any guy could have and now lives in Canada.

After leaving the army, what to do?

I decided to go to university and study a BCom. I went to the University of the Witwatersrand (WITS) and studied for three years. I was not the best student, but I did pass and get my degree. Thereafter, I completed my Honours degree in finance at The university of South Africa (Unisa).


Work


I spent many years working for various companies in the role of Financial Manager.

I was never really happy or satisfied but then oneday quite by accident I discovered programming (Or Coding). As the financial manager for a group of companies, I asked my IT manager to create some reports for me, I was informed it is not possible, I did not believe that and as a result taught myself how to code.

That was the start of my journey into the world of coding. I have never looked back and have never regretted my decision to change careers. I was fortunate enough to be able to work for a company that allowed me to code as my full-time job and I have never looked back.

My Aspirations: To be 100% remote and to be able to work from anywhere in the world.
